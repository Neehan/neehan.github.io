Hi, I'm Adib Hasan. I build [long-horizon agents](https://github.com/SignalPilot-Labs/AutoFyn) that improve through [expert iteration](https://arxiv.org/abs/1705.08439) in context space, using rich feedback from verifiers, tools, and real-world execution.

Using this approach, my agents have discovered 150+ vulnerabilities in widely used OSS projects, including Next.js, pnpm, and MetaMask; improved the upper bound for [an open math problem](https://github.com/Neehan/zhang-zagier-82a); and topped the [Spider 2.0 DBT benchmark](https://github.com/SignalPilot-Labs/SignalPilot) for data science tasks. Previously, I spent two years as a **quant developer** managing multi-million dollar crypto portfolios and leading a team of four. You can find more on my [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/adib-hasan).

I completed my bachelor's in Math & EECS and MEng in EECS at MIT, where I was advised by [Dr. Mardavij Roozbehani](https://idss.mit.edu/staff/mardavij-roozbehani/) and [Prof. Munther Dahleh](https://idss.mit.edu/staff/munther-dahleh/). My research focused on model robustness under distribution shift, resulting in two papers: a [variational pretraining framework](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.03589) for weather time series (AAAI 2026 Oral), and a [pruning-based jailbreak defense](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.10862) for LLMs (EMNLP Workshop 2024).

I grew up in Bangladesh, where I spent much of high school training for and participating in the International Mathematical Olympiad. I also compiled a collection of my olympiad preparation notes from those years [here](https://adibhasan.com/files/adib_olympiad_prep.pdf).

Beyond work, I cook passionately to avoid starvation ([proof](https://www.instagram.com/le.spicemaster/)), read [dead philosophers](/reading/), and lift [heavy objects calmly yet furiously](/powerlifting/). I also co-authored two [math textbooks](/portfolio/) and built a free [college admissions GPT](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-673c242ba900819183752398e7a8833b-essay-mate). Both make high school students cry, but for completely different reasons.
